Place these DigitalPersona runtime files here with these exact names:

websdk.client.bundle.min.js
websdk.compat.js
dp.core.bundle.js
dp.devices.bundle.js

Expected final paths:
frontend/public/websdk.client.bundle.min.js
frontend/public/websdk.compat.js
frontend/public/dp.core.bundle.js
frontend/public/dp.devices.bundle.js

`websdk.client.bundle.min.js` comes from the DigitalPersona SDK/client installation.
`websdk.compat.js` is included in this project and patches older WebSdk runtime APIs.
`dp.core.bundle.js` and `dp.devices.bundle.js` can be copied from:
frontend/node_modules/@digitalpersona/core/dist/es5.bundles/index.umd.js
frontend/node_modules/@digitalpersona/devices/dist/es5.bundles/index.umd.js
